# Organization Settings

## Overview

The Organization Settings allow administrators to configure and manage organization-wide settings within SalesAstra. These settings establish the company identity, operational preferences, branding standards, regional configurations, and work schedules used across the platform.

Organization Settings ensure consistency across:

- Billing and invoicing
- CRM communications
- Workflows and automations
- Reporting and dashboards
- SLA calculations
- User-facing branding

:::note
Only users with Admin or authorized permissions can access and modify these settings.
:::

---

## Accessing Organization Settings

Navigate to:

**Settings → Organization Settings**

The Organization Settings module contains the following sections:

- Profile
- Localization
- Work Schedule
- Branding

---

# 1. Organization Profile

The **Organization Profile** section stores the company’s core business information and primary contact details.

## Purpose

This information is used across multiple modules including:

- Billing and invoices
- CRM-generated emails
- Legal and compliance references
- Reports and exports
- Organization identity display

---

![organization profile](/img/OrganizationSettings/orgprofile.png)

## Company Details

### Company Name

This name is used throughout the CRM for organizational identity.

### Legal Company Name

Optional field used for:

- Invoices
- Contracts
- Compliance documents
- Legal references

### Industry

Administrators can select from predefined industries.

#### Examples

- Information Technology
- Healthcare
- Manufacturing
- Education

### Company Size

Select the employee range that best represents the organization.

#### Examples

- 1–10
- 11–50
- 51–200
- 201–500
- 500+

### Website URL

Enter the organization's official website address.

---

## Address Configuration

### Registered Address

Store the official business address.

#### Fields Include

- Address Line 1
- Address Line 2
- City
- State
- Country
- ZIP / Postal Code

---

### Billing Address

Administrators can choose:

- Same as Registered Address
- Separate Billing Address

:::note
If Separate Billing Address is selected, additional address fields become available.
:::

---

## Primary Contact (POC) Details

The **Primary Point of Contact (POC)** represents the organization's main business contact.

The POC information is used for:

- Administrative communications
- Billing-related notifications
- Account management interactions
- Customer success and support communications
