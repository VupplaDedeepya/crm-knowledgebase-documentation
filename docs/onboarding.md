## Onboarding Overview

The **onboarding process** in **Sales Astra CRM** includes:

1. **User Registration**
2. **Account Verification**
3. **Workspace Creation** or Selection
4. **Team Member Invitation**
5. **Dashboard Access**

---

### Registration Methods

Users can register into **Sales Astra CRM** using any of the following methods:

- **Email Registration**
- **Google Sign-In**
- **Microsoft Sign-In**

---

### Step 1: Open Sales Astra CRM

Launch the **Sales Astra CRM** application using the provided application URL.

![Open CRM](/img/onboarding/open-crm.png)

---

### Step 2: Click on “Sign Up”

Click on the Sign-Up option to begin the registration process.

![Signup Page](/img/onboarding/signup-page.png)

---

### Step 3: Choose Registration Method

Select one of the available signup methods:

- Continue with Email
- Continue with Google
- Continue with Microsoft

![Registration Method](/img/onboarding/registration-method.png)

---

### Step 4: Enter User Information

Provide the required details such as:

- Full Name
- Email Address
- Password

#### Password Requirements

- Minimum 12 characters
- At least one lowercase letter
- At least one uppercase letter
- At least one number or special symbol

After entering the required details, click **Create Account**.

![User Information](/img/onboarding/user-information.png)

---

### Step 5: Verify Account

Users may be required to verify their account through an email verification link or OTP.

![Verify Account](/img/onboarding/verify-account.png)

---

### Step 6: Name Your Workspace

Create a **unique workspace name** for your **CRM environment**.

#### Why is a Unique Workspace Required in CRM?

- Ensures **secure separation** of company data
- Provides a **personalized workspace** for each organization
- Supports **role-based access** and permissions
- Improves **team collaboration** and communication
- Allows **custom workflows** and dashboards
- Helps organize **leads, tasks, and customer data** efficiently
- Supports **scalability** as the business grows
- Enables **organization-specific reporting** and analytics
- Maintains **customer data privacy** and security
- Creates a **structured and efficient** CRM environment

![Workspace Name](/img/onboarding/workspace-name.png)

---

### Step 7: Enter Company Details

Provide company name and optional company website information.

![Company Details](/img/onboarding/company-details.png)

---

### Step 8: Select Company Size

Choose the company size from the available options.

![Company Size](/img/onboarding/company-size.png)

---

### Step 9: Select Your Role

Choose the role that best matches your position within the organization.

![User Role](/img/onboarding/user-role.png)

---

### Step 10: Referral Information

Select how you heard about Sales Astra CRM.

![Referral Information](/img/onboarding/referral-info.png)

---

### Step 11: Select Industry Type

Choose the industry category that best represents your organization.

![Industry Type](/img/onboarding/industry-type.png)

---

### Step 12: Connect Business Tools

Optionally integrate commonly used business tools.

![Business Tools](/img/onboarding/business-tools.png)

---

### Step 13: Invite Team Members

Invite teammates to join the workspace. This step is optional.

![Invite Team Members](/img/onboarding/invite-team.png)

---

### Step 14: Access the Dashboard

After completing the onboarding process, users will be redirected to the Sales Astra CRM dashboard.

Users can now begin:

- Managing leads
- Tracking customer interactions
- Creating tasks and meetings
- Collaborating with team members
- Managing business workflows
