# Pulse Settings

## Overview

Pulse is the customer engagement and support platform within SalesAstra that enables businesses to interact with website visitors, leads, and customers through chat, automation, AI-powered responses, and human support teams.

Pulse Settings allow administrators to configure how the chatbot operates, how conversations are routed, who can access the platform, when support is available, and what knowledge sources the AI can use to assist customers.

These settings help organizations:

- Deliver consistent customer support experiences
- Automate responses to common queries
- Route conversations to the right agents
- Manage support team access and permissions
- Define business availability hours
- Maintain a centralized knowledge repository
- Configure chatbot behavior across channels

---

## Pulse Settings Modules

The Pulse Settings module consists of the following sections:

### Channels

Configure communication channels through which customers can interact with your business, such as:

- Website chat widgets
- Messaging platforms
- Social channels
- Other supported communication channels

#### Purpose

- Enable customer communication across multiple channels
- Centralize conversations within Pulse
- Improve customer accessibility and engagement

---

### Users & Access

Manage support agents, administrators, roles, permissions, and access control for the Pulse platform.

#### Capabilities

- Add and manage support agents
- Configure user roles
- Assign permissions
- Control platform access
- Manage administrative privileges

#### Purpose

- Ensure secure platform access
- Maintain role-based permissions
- Support team management and accountability

---

### Assignment Rules

Define how incoming conversations are automatically assigned to agents or teams based on predefined conditions and routing logic.

#### Capabilities

- Automatic conversation routing
- Team-based assignment
- Rule-based distribution
- Workload balancing

#### Purpose

- Reduce manual assignment effort
- Improve response times
- Ensure conversations reach the appropriate team members

---

### Knowledge Base

Create and manage articles, FAQs, and information sources that can be used by both AI-powered chatbots and support agents.

#### Supported Content

- Help articles
- Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)
- Troubleshooting guides
- Product documentation
- Internal knowledge resources

#### Purpose

- Improve self-service support
- Provide accurate AI responses
- Enable faster agent assistance
- Maintain a centralized information repository

---

### Support Availability

Set business hours, agent availability schedules, holidays, and support operating times to manage customer expectations.

#### Configuration Options

- Business hours
- Agent schedules
- Holiday calendars
- Availability settings
- Support operating times

#### Purpose

- Define support coverage
- Set customer expectations
- Manage response availability
- Support SLA compliance

---

### Global Settings

Configure organization-wide Pulse preferences, chatbot behavior, notification settings, branding, and other default system configurations.

#### Configuration Areas

- Chatbot behavior
- Notification preferences
- Branding settings
- Default system configurations
- Organization-wide Pulse preferences

#### Purpose

- Standardize Pulse behavior across the organization
- Maintain consistent customer experiences
- Simplify platform administration

---

## Benefits of Pulse Settings

Properly configuring Pulse Settings helps organizations:

- Deliver efficient customer support
- Improve response times
- Increase customer satisfaction
- Automate repetitive interactions
- Maintain consistent support processes
- Optimize agent productivity
- Enhance AI-powered customer engagement
