# Workflows

## Overview

Workflows enable organizations to automate repetitive business processes and reduce manual effort.

By defining a series of triggers, conditions, and actions, workflows ensure that tasks, communications, and updates are executed automatically whenever specific events occur.

Workflow automation helps teams maintain consistency, improve response times, eliminate human error, and ensure that important activities are never missed.

## Organizations Can Automate

- Sending emails, WhatsApp messages, or SMS notifications
- Creating and assigning follow-up tasks
- Updating Lead records automatically
- Routing Leads to appropriate teams
- Logging activities and system-generated notes
- Triggering actions based on Lead status, stage changes, or scheduled events

## Benefits of Workflows

### Improved Response Time

Automatically engage Leads as soon as they enter the system.

### Consistent Sales Processes

Ensure every Lead follows the same qualification and follow-up process.

### Increased Productivity

Reduce manual effort through automation.

### Better Accountability

Automatically create and track tasks.

### Enhanced Visibility

Maintain a complete audit trail of workflow actions.

### Scalability

Handle large Lead volumes efficiently.
