# Knowledge Base

## Overview

The Knowledge Base allows administrators to provide company-specific information that the Pulse Chatbot can use when responding to website visitors.

By adding documents and website content, organizations can ensure that the chatbot provides accurate, relevant, and context-aware responses based on their business information.

The chatbot uses this knowledge alongside configured conversation flows to answer customer questions, provide information, and assist visitors more effectively.

---

## Purpose

The Knowledge Base helps the chatbot:

- Answer customer questions using company-specific information.
- Provide consistent and accurate responses.
- Reduce dependency on human agents for common inquiries.
- Improve customer self-service experiences.
- Support AI-generated responses with trusted business content.

## Upload Documents

Administrators can upload documents that contain information relevant to customers, products, services, policies, or support processes.

### Supported File Formats

- PDF
- DOC / DOCX
- TXT

Each uploaded file becomes part of the chatbot's knowledge source and can be referenced when generating responses.

### Examples of Documents

- Product documentation
- User guides
- FAQs
- Company policies
- Support articles
- Service information
- Training materials

:::note
Uploaded documents are indexed and made available to the chatbot as searchable knowledge sources.
:::

---

## Add Knowledge URL

Instead of uploading documents, administrators can add website URLs to allow the system to index content directly from web pages.

This is useful when information already exists on the company website and does not need to be maintained as separate documents.

### Examples

- Help Center pages
- Product documentation websites
- FAQ pages
- Support portals
- Knowledge Base articles

Once added, the content from the URL becomes available to the chatbot when generating responses.

---

## Website Content and Subpages

When indexing website content, administrators can include important website sections and subpages to provide broader knowledge coverage.

### Examples

- Product pages
- Support pages
- FAQ sections
- Documentation pages
- Footer links
- Header navigation pages
- Contact pages
- Policy pages

:::tip
Including relevant subpages helps ensure the chatbot can answer a wider range of customer questions.
:::

---

## Tags

Administrators can assign tags to uploaded documents and indexed content.

Tags help organize knowledge sources and improve content categorization.

### Example Tags

- Products
- Pricing
- Billing
- Support
- Onboarding
- Policies
- Integrations

### Benefits of Tags

- Improve content organization
- Simplify knowledge management
- Enhance content discoverability
- Help AI identify relevant information more accurately

:::note
Using meaningful tags makes it easier to manage knowledge content and helps the AI identify relevant information when responding to customer queries.
:::

---

## How the Chatbot Uses the Knowledge Base

When a visitor asks a question, the chatbot searches the available knowledge sources to identify relevant information.

The chatbot may use:

- Uploaded documents
- Indexed website content
- Tagged knowledge sources
- Configured conversation flows

Based on the available information, the chatbot generates a response that is relevant to the visitor's query.

---

## Best Practices

- Keep documents and website content up to date.
- Use descriptive file names and tags.
- Regularly review indexed URLs for accuracy.
- Organize content into meaningful categories.
- Remove outdated or duplicate knowledge sources.
- Ensure uploaded content reflects current business information.

:::tip
For best results, ensure that all knowledge sources accurately reflect your organization's latest products, services, policies, and support information.
:::
